BUILD SMART APPS

Harness the power of big data (both yours and third-parties) to build apps that are intelligent and insightful.

Our cloud-scalable infrastructure stores all your transactional data, enriches it with third-party data that you choose, and gives you powerful APIs to analyze that data so you can integrate intelligence and insights into the apps you build.

HOW IT WORKS

RAW DATA IN

Use our data storage API to capture huge amounts of transactional information from your app: user actions, system changes, and so forth. We accept JSON so you can send strings, numbers, arrays, objects, data from a relational database, or tree-like data.

MARKETPLACE

Use our control panel to decide how you want to enrich your data. Demographics by email or IP? Check. Sentiment analysis or concept extraction? Check. Census, weather, finance? Check. Your data is awesome. Make it even more awesome with our marketplace.

INSIGHT OUT

Use our analysis API to crunch terabytes of your data and third-party data. Our analysis API makes short work of jobs that would be impossible in SQL or take weeks in MapReduce. You get to focus on solving your problem, we handle all the messy technical details!

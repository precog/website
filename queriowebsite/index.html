BUILD SMART APPS

Precog is a big data analytics platform that helps you build apps that are intelligent and insightful.

With our simple APIs, you can store, enrich, and analyze massive amounts of multi-structured data to power mind-blowing features in your apps.

HOW IT WORKS

RAW DATA IN

1

Use our data storage API to capture huge amounts of structured or unstructured data from your app or any other data source.

MARKETPLACE

2

Choose add-ons from the marketplace to enrich your data: geo, demographics, sentiment analysis, social profiles, and others.

INTELLIGENCE OUT

3

Use our data analysis API inside your app to power real-time intelligence, recommendations, insights, predictions, and more.

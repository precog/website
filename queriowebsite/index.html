What Does Querio Do?

Querio is a platform that helps you build intelligent, insightful, data-driven apps. Our platform lets you capture data, enrich it with add-ons (like sentiment analysis or demographics), and analyze the information with a high-level analytical query language. Querio is hosted in the cloud, so there's nothing for you to install, maintain, or configure.

How Can Querio Help?

If your app needs to make intelligent decisions based on the analysis of large amounts of data, or surface deep insights to end-users, then Querio can help. Using our powerful API, Querio can capture and enrich your data, and provide statistics, insights, recommendations, trends, clustering, segmentation, optimization, predictions, and more.

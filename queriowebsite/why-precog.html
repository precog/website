WHY PRECOG?

What is Precog?

Precog is a platform that helps developers capture, enrich, and analyze massive amounts of multi-structured data, to power insights and intelligence inside their applications.

What do I get from Precog that I can't do myself?

Precog is a cloud platform, so there's nothing for you to install or maintain. The service scales to meet your needs, whether you have a small amount of data or a large amount of data, a small number of queries or a large number of queries.

Precog offers an integrated marketplace for data cleaning, enrichment, and processing. With a few clicks in your control panel, you can take any data you're storing in Precog and clean or enrich it with amazing add-ons like sentiment analysis, demographics, and facets.

Finally, Precog offers extremely sophisticated analytics and statistics. While you could build these features on top of a data store like Hadoop or MongoDB, it would take a long time and a lot of effort, and making it as fast as Precog is not feasible for most companies.

This combination of features gives you a tremendous amount of functionality with virtually no effort. We don't solve your problems for you, but we do make it easier for you to focus on your problems, rather than the technology that so often gets out of the way. This lets you deliver more value for less money, and have more fun while you're doing it.

Who is using Precog?

Precog is not designed for end-users. It's designed for developers who are building data-driven applications. We help developers make their applications intelligent, insightful — even precognizant.

Precog's customers include FlixMaster, SnapEngage, ThinkNear, and many others.

Why choose Precog?

Precog gives you a tremendous edge in big data analytics. You get all the power you need to make intelligent, insightful apps, without having to pay the cost in blood, sweat, and tears — and you get it for a fraction of the time and money it would take you to build in-house.

Our platform is 100% free to try, we don't have any long-term commitments, and you can migrate your data off our platform at any time for no charge.

So why not give Precog a try today?

Precog has a super simple API. No SOAP here, just pure REST + JSON.

Our deep analysis API goes beyond sums and counts and lets you do sophisticated analytics and statistics.

Your data is hosted on beefy dedicated machines, on 100% solid state storage, with continuous real-time backups.

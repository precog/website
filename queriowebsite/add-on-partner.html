ADD-ON PARTNERS

Precog is the premier platform for building data-driven apps. A key component of Precog is the add-on marketplace, which contains a growing collection of add-ons designed to enrich, clean, and process data.

We are actively looking for valuable add-ons in a wide range of categories:

  • Natural language processing
  • Demographics
  • Business data
  • Local data
  • Weather
  • Census
  • Geo data
  • Data cleaning
  • Data verification
  • And lots more...

If you have an interesting data source or data processing service, then we'd love to talk to you. Although you may already have an API, Precog offers a new distribution channel, and benefits that go beyond API distribution models (such as monthly recurring revenue, control over the data, etc.).

Interested? Then contact us to get started today!

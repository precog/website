ReportGrid makes it easy to do turn raw data into insight

How do you make sense of terrabytes or petabytes of data? You need two things: a database that can deliver compelling analytics on even large amounts of data, and a visualization engine that can make insights pop out.

At ReportGrid, we package up a proprietary analytics database and visualization engine into one shrink-wrapped box, hosted in the cloud and able to scale to meet your changing needs. We take the pain out of analytics and reporting, so you can focus on mining your data for insight rather than the merchanics of storage, sharding, indexes, aggregates, and report generation.

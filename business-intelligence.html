Custom, automated BI solutions through our API

If you're in the market for a business intelligence solution, you have two choices: buy a high-level tool for your data analysts, who then need to import data from all your company's sources of data, and manually generate queries and reports; or build an automated solution, tailor-made for your company, which automatically pulls in all the right data and generates all the right views.

If you've decided on the automated solution, then the ReportGrid API can help save you time and money. With our API, it's trivial for developers to hook up one or a dozen different sources of data, and bring deep analysis of these data sources together in a real-time dashboard that helps you make the best business decisions.

No free development resources? No problem — we offer a complete line of professional services targeted at helping you realize your goals on top of the ReportGrid platform.

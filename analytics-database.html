The features you need for today and for tomorrow

Our analytics database handles more than just big data. With a growing list of features backed by ReportGrid's engineering team, you can count on us to satisfy your needs — the ones you have now, and the ones you will have tomorrow.

  • Full Stack Solution

    We solve both the big data analytics problem, and the visualization problem.

  • Analytics

    At any scale our analytics technology can handle massive amounts of data. We shard, replicate, and index data so you don't have to.

  • Visualizations, Not Reports

    Most reports are static. Our visualizations are highly interactive and engaging, across web and mobile.

  • Beyond Numbers

    Our API is designed to turn numbers into insights that help your customers make better decisions based on real data.

  • Geo

    Our backend has built-in support for geo data (either user- defined or auto-detected by IP) and visualizations.

  • Time Series

    Our backend has robust support for time series data, from minute granularity up to the year and beyond.

  • Real-Time Insight

    Our query engine speeds up common queries so that your customers don't have to wait for the analytics they need.

  • High-Level API

    Our easy API lets you focus on the problems you're trying to solve, not the mechanics of how to solve them.

  • Always Improving

    Our world-class engineers never stop working on improving our technology, so you benefit from the latest advances.

  • Professional Services

    We can help you get the most out of your data, and work to implement a robust solution on top of our platform.
